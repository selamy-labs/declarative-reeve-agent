# Career Opportunity Triage

Use this skill to turn career inbound into a private pipeline update and a next-action draft.

## Procedure

1. Identify the source, company, role, contact, and any deadline or scheduling request.
2. Classify the inbound as one of the workflow decision classes: `reply-needed`, `scheduling`, `prep-only`, `resume-needed`, `application-receipt`, `closure`, `passive-review`, or `noise`.
3. Assign the pipeline state: `new-lead`, `qualification-needed`, `resume-requested`, `scheduling-needed`, `booked-prep`, `assessment`, `interview-loop`, `awaiting-counterpart`, or `closed`.
4. Prefer qualification before commitment when level, compensation, working model, interview format, or role scope is missing.
5. Suppress duplicate notifications when a newer thread or calendar state already represents the same opportunity.
6. Produce a private pipeline row and, when useful, a draft next action.

## Guardrails

- Do not send or schedule anything directly.
- Do not infer compensation, level, location, or work authorization facts that are not present in the evidence.
- Treat third-party names, messages, and transcript excerpts as private unless policy explicitly allows use.
