# Interview Prep Packet

Use this skill to turn opportunity context into grounded prep artifacts.

## Procedure

1. Gather role, company, interview stage, interviewers, job description, prior thread context, and relevant career evidence.
2. Build a target packet with role thesis, likely evaluation criteria, matching evidence, risk areas, questions to ask, and follow-up notes.
3. Create a resume-variant checklist when the role needs a tailored resume, not a fabricated resume.
4. After an interview or call, ingest transcript or notes into a debrief with facts, commitments, risks, next steps, and follow-up draft needs.
5. Update private state with artifact links and pipeline status.

## Guardrails

- Do not invent interviewers, company facts, role requirements, or career evidence.
- Keep raw transcripts private.
- Public or shareable excerpts require privacy validation and explicit approval.
